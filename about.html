About Us

ORFLAX: Your Partner in Electrical Excellence

Orflax India Pvt. Ltd. is a North Indian company specializing in the manufacture and supply of electric wires and cables under the "Orflax" brand. With over 15 years of experience, they produce power and control cables, lighting cables, and industrial cables, adhering to ISO 9001-2015 standards. Their products undergo rigorous quality checks, and they are known for innovations like triple-layer coating. The company serves a wide range of customers and has earned several industry awards.

Why Choose ORFLAX?

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Quality: Our commitment to quality is demonstrated by our adherence to the rigorous standards outlined in ISO 9001:2015. Our quality management system ensures consistent quality throughout our operations, from raw material procurement to final product delivery. By meeting international quality standards, we consistently deliver products that exceed customer expectations.
  • Comprehensive Product Range: Our wide range of electric wires and cables caters to various industries and applications, from residential and commercial buildings to industrial facilities. We offer products suitable for different power requirements, voltage levels, and environmental conditions. Customers can find all their electrical wire and cable needs under one roof, simplifying their procurement process.
  • Triple Layer Coating: The triple layer coating provides superior protection against corrosion, abrasion, and other environmental factors. This advanced coating ensures that our cables have a longer lifespan and can withstand demanding conditions. The triple layer coating enhances the overall reliability and performance of our cables
  • 15 Years of Excellence: Our extensive experience in the electrical industry has equipped us with valuable knowledge and insights. Our 15-year history of successful operations demonstrates our ability to deliver quality products and services. Our long-standing presence in the market has earned us the trust and confidence of our customers.
  • Customization and Quality Assurance: We work closely with customers to understand their specific needs and provide customized solutions. Our products undergo stringent quality testing at every stage of the manufacturing process to ensure they meet the highest standards. We are committed to continually improving our processes and products to meet evolving customer requirements.
  • North Indian Leader: Our position as a leading business group in North India gives us a deep understanding of local market dynamics. We leverage our local expertise to provide efficient and effective solutions to our customers. Our strong network of suppliers and distributors enables us to offer competitive pricing and timely delivery.
